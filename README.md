# AABIM
Atmospheric Aquatic Bayesian Inversion Model
```
    A         A     BBBB  IIIII MM   MM
   A A       A A    B   B   I   M M M M
  A   A     A   A   B BB    I   M  M  M
 A A A A   A A A A  B   B   I   M     M
A       A A       A BBBB  IIIII M     M
```

[![Code style: black](https://img.shields.io/badge/code%20style-black-000000.svg)](https://github.com/psf/black)

This software integrate atmospheric and aquatic models for parameters estimation with Bayesian inversion. Results achieved with this software can be found in the Raphael Mabit 2026 thesis, *Réflectances d’Anticosti: radiométrie in situ, imagerie hyperspectrale aéroportée et
inversion bayésienne en eaux optiquement peu profondes*.

Use arviz for MC model inspection ? https://python.arviz.org/en/stable/
